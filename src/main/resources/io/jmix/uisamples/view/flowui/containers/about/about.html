Layouts are components that can contain other components. They are used to arrange components on a view.

vbox and hbox are basic layouts that arrange their content vertically or horizontally.

formLayout is a responsive layout that arranges components into columns and shows field labels. The number of columns depends on the screen width.

tabSheet and accordion split content into sections and allow users to switch between them.

details is a panel that can hide its content.

scroller can scroll its content if it does not fit the size of the container.

split creates two content areas divided by a draggable splitter.
