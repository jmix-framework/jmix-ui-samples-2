A fragment can subscribe to some events in its lifecycle, as well as in the lifecycle of its host-view.
The following events are available for subscription:

  • Fragment.ReadyEvent
  • View.InitEvent (host)
  • View.BeforeShowEvent (host)
  • View.ReadyEvent (host)

Fragment.ReadyEvent - an event that is fired after the fragment and all its declaratively defined inner components are created, fully initialized and dependency injection completed. Event subscriptions are implemented in such a way that the listeners defined in the host-view are executed first, and then in the fragment.

Note that when creating the fragment programmatically, the View.InitEvent event listener for host-view was not executed. This happened because at that moment the fragment was created and the View.InitEvent event has already fired.
